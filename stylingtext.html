wise man story

people have been coming to the wise man,complaining about the same problems every time. one day he told them a joke and everyone roared in laughter. after a couple of minutes,he told them the same joke and only a few of them smiled. when he told same joke for the third time no one laughed anymore.
The wise man smiled and said
:"you can't laugh at the same joke over and over. so why are you always crying about the same problem?"
